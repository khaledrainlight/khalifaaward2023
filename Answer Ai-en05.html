Requirements for applying to Educational Research Field are:


• The research material must not have been prepared as part of acquiring a Bachelor’s, Master’s, or PhD degree or equivalent.
• Such research should not even include content from other Master’s, PhD dissertations or equivalent work in the research submitted for the Award.
• The research should not have been instructed by an official administration, such as a university, research center, institute, or ministry.
• The research should not have been previously published, or in the process of publishing whether in a book or a refereed journal.
